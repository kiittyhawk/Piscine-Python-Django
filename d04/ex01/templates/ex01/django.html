Django History

Before we dive into the study of the code, we should pay attention to a little time of Django development history. We noted that we we will tell you how everything works without auxiliary tools to help you deal with internal kitchen processes. Similarly, it will be useful for understanding why the environment was created Django, why Django works this way and not otherwise.

If you have sufficient application development experience, maybe have you encountered the problems shown in the CGI example, which we demonstrated earlier. The classic process application development looks like this:

  1. Create an application from scratch.

  2. Create another application from scratch.

  3. Implement the work of the first application with the second.

  4. Rework the code so that both applications use common functions.

  5. Repeat steps 2-4 several times.

  6. You invented the development environment.

That's exactly how Django was created!

Django grew organically during the development of real applications, created by a development team in Lawrence, Kansas, USA. She was born at the end of 2003 when the programmers of the newspaper "Lawrence Journal-World", Adrian Holovati (Adrian Holovaty) and Simon Willison (Simon Willison), started using the Python language for development their applications. The World Online team responsible for the development and support of several local news sites flourished in the developer environment dictated by the urgency of work a journalist. For sites including LJWorld.com , Lawrence.com and KUsports.com journalists and managers demanded that new features and entire applications be developed as much as possible quickly, often the bill went for days or hours. Thus, Simon and Adrian created an environment time-saving development based on their needs — this was the only way they could create managed applications in strict deadlines.

In the summer of 2005, having developed this environment to such a state that it supported most sites World Online, a team of developers, including Jacob Kaplan-Moss, decided release the environment as open source software. They let him out In July 2005, under the name Django in honor of jazz guitarist Django Reinhardt (Django Reinhardt).

Now, after several years, Django is literate an open source project with tens of thousands users that developers are working on all over the the planet. Two authors from the World Online team ("Generous lifelong dictators", Adrian and Jacob) all still carry out general guidance on the development of the development environment, but now it to a greater extent, it depends on the joint efforts of the development team. World Online provides other important aspects such as time developers, marketing materials and hosting/channel for the environment website.

This story is relevant because it helps explain two key aspects. The first aspect — the main purpose of Django. So as Django was born in a news environment, it provides some tools (such as the administrative interface described in chapter " Admin Interface Django"), which are good suitable for content-oriented sites such as Amazon.com , craigslist.org and washingtonpost.com , which provide dynamic information from the database. Exists the difference between being particularly effective in one way and being ineffective in another.

The second aspect is how Django's origins shaped the culture of its community open source code. Since Django was derived from real code, and was not academic development or commercial product, it is fully focused on the solution development problems that its authors have faced and continue to face. As a result, Django is constantly being improved. The founders of the environment have their own interest in Django saving their time, creating easy-to-maintain applications and working well under load. In the absence of other reasons, developers are motivated by their own selfish desires to save your time and enjoy your work.
